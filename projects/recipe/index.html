How to craft a potion of invisibility

This Minecraft tutorial explains how to craft a Potion of Invisibility with screenshots and step-by-step instructions.

In Minecraft, you can brew a Potion of Invisibility and add it to your inventory. When this potion is drank, it will give you the Invisibility effect so that no one can see you.

Supported Platforms

  • Java
  • Pocket edition
  • Xbox
  • Nintendo Switch
  • Windows 10 Edition
  • Education Edition

Required Materials

  • Brewing stand
  • 3 glass (or 1 glass bottle)
  • 1 nether wart
  • 3 blaze powder
  • 1 golden carrot
  • 1 spider eye
  • 1 sugar
  • 1 brown mushroom

Optional materials

  • 1 redstone dust
  • 1 Dragon's Breath
  • 1 Gunpowder

Instructions

Now that you have all your ingredients, it is time to prepare your materials for brewing!!

Prepare materials

  1. If you already have a water bottle, skip to step 3. Otherwise, use a crafting table and place 3 glass to create a glass bottle
  2. Right click any water source with glass bottle to fill it up
  3. Craft a fermented spider eye by placing a sugar, brown mushroom, and spider eye in any orientation

Brewing

  1. Open the Brewing Stand menu by right clicking on the brewing stand
  2. Add Blaze Powder by adding it to the far left box
  3. Activate the bottle by placing the bottle(s) and netherwart in the designated spots
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 with the golden carrot, then the fermented spider eye
  5. Enjoy your invisbility by holding right click to drink!

Tips and Tricks

Your invisbility potionn has a variety of uses and secrets about it. Here are a few expert tips:

Make it last longer by using redstone dust to increase the duration from 3 to 8 minutes!

Create a splash effect by using gunpowder, making it easier to splash on yourself instead of having to drink it!

Make other potions by referring to this guide!
